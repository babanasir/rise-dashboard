📖 How to Connect Your Google Sheet
  1. Open your Google Sheet with materials data
  2. Go to File → Share → Publish to web
  3. Choose "Entire document" or select specific sheet
  4. Select "Comma-separated values (.csv)" format
  5. Click "Publish"
  6. Copy the published URL (it should contain "pub?output=csv")
  7. Paste it in the field above and click "Connect to Sheet"
Required columns: Material_ID, Material Type, Language, Grade Level, Status
Material Type values: Teacher Guide, Decodable Text, Leveled Reader, Illustrations
Grade Level values: ECD 2, ECD 3, Grade 1, Grade 2, Grade 3
Language values: Fula, Jola, Mandinka, Manjaku, Serahule, Serer, Wolof, English
Status values: 1st Draft Ready, Internal Review Ongoing, Internal Review Completed
